Meet our team.

Lawrence Chang

Lawrence Chang is a licensed Texas attorney born and raised in Austin, Texas. After receiving his undergraduate degree from the University of Texas at Austin, he attended the University of Illinois Law School in Champaign Urbana to obtain his law degree. Immediately after graduation he went on to work as a Assistant District Attorney in Dallas County until 2015, when finally his heart called him back to Austin and he found a new home with Choate & Associates. Having accrued a wealth of litigation and courtroom experience throughout his career, Lawrence prides himself on his ability, and proven history, of picking winning juries.

During my time with the District Attorney, I came to the understanding that the legal system is broken, especially here in Texas. Privatized prisons have created perverse incentives to keep our citizens behind bars instead of rehabilitating them and reintroducing them as productive members of society. Civilly there have been massive financial barriers put in place to prevent the less fortunate from protecting or asserting their legal rights. Costs of court, attorneys fees, costs to file, service fees, and the list goes on and on. I believe that everybody deserves justice. I believe that everybody deserves a second chance. There is no case too big or small. If it matters to you, it matters to me, and I will fight to get you the justice you deserve.

Lawrence Chang
